# Cookie Policy — Praxia

**Effective date**: TO BE FINALIZED

This policy explains how Praxia websites use cookies and similar storage
technologies.

## What we use

The Praxia website (`praxia.dev`, `praxia.pages.dev`,
`genarch.github.io/praxia`) uses **minimal browser storage**:

| Technology | Purpose | Required? | Retention |
|---|---|---|---|
| `sessionStorage` (`praxia-mirror-dismissed`) | Remember that you dismissed the "switch to faster mirror" banner | No | Until tab closed |
| Hosting provider cookies (Cloudflare / GitHub Pages) | Security / DDoS protection | Yes (necessary) | Per provider's policy |

We do **not** use:

- Advertising cookies
- Cross-site tracking cookies
- Third-party analytics that profile individuals

## Optional analytics (planned)

If we add web analytics in the future, we will use a privacy-friendly
solution (e.g., Cloudflare Web Analytics, Plausible) that does not
require cookies and does not track individuals. We will update this
policy and present a clear opt-in / opt-out before activating any new
tracking.

## Hosted portal / Service

The Praxia portal (sign-in / sign-up) and any Provider-operated hosted
Service may use additional cookies for authentication, session
management, and security. These are described in
[Privacy Policy § 2](PRIVACY.md#2-what-we-collect).

## How to control cookies

Most browsers allow you to:

- View and delete cookies
- Block cookies from specific sites
- Block all cookies (note: parts of the Service may not function)

See your browser's documentation for instructions.
